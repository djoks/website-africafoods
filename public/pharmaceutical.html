About Africa Foods
This is who we are.

AFRICO aims to strive to become the largest indigenous fresh food producer and distributor in Ghana and Africa. Our mission is to be the most sustainable producer and reliable supplier of consistent, high quality livestock feed, fresh pork, beef and chicken in the country, demonstrating a "Culture of Sustainability" in everything we do, and creating value for our shareholders, customers, team members and communities.

Most of our products will initially be sold in Ghana and expand to other West African countries. We also aim to maintain efficient, state-of-the-art operations and farms located close to our customers.

We were founded in June 2021 by Nana Edusah and are headquartered in Prah Ewusi, Central Region. The Company has five operating segments, which is the production, grading, packaging, marketing and distribution of premium livestock feed, beef, pork, chicken and other consumer products.

Our Strategy Sustainably produce and source and supply the best quality food and beverages to help accelerate development in Africa.

How we will achieve our strategy:

Grow our business by delivering superior value to consumers and customers.

Deliver fuel for growth and returns through commercial, operational and financial excellence.

Sustain Our company and our world for future generations.
